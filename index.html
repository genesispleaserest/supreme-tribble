FAQ

Why choose n8n vs Zapier?

Zapier is fast for standard SaaS actions and simple paths. n8n shines for complex branching, custom code, ETL, and on‑prem/self‑host control. We recommend the right tool per workflow—and often use both.

Can you work with our stack?

Yes. We integrate most CRMs, marketing suites, data stores, and internal tools. For custom systems, we bridge via REST/GraphQL, webhooks, or queues.

Do you offer maintenance?

We provide monitoring, alerting, and break‑fix retainers. Options include monthly health checks and upgrade cycles.
